Technology direction

Advanced nonwoven sleeve development.

Sealroll focuses on nonwoven sleeve structures designed for demanding strip processing environments. The goal is to improve surface quality, wiping performance and dimensional stability under industrial operating conditions.

  • Reduced need for repeated strip wiping
  • High compression stability
  • Wear and chemical resistance
  • Regrindable roller sleeve concepts
  • Local development for import substitution
